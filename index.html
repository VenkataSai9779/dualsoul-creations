Our Story

We are Kishore and Charan, two passionate dancers who came together to create something extraordinary. DualSoul Creations is born from our shared love for dance and our desire to express emotions through movement.


Our journey began with a simple idea - to create dance content that resonates with people, tells stories, and inspires others to follow their passion. Every reel we create is a piece of our soul, crafted with dedication and love for the art form.


Join us on this beautiful journey as we explore various dance styles, push our creative boundaries, and build a community of dance enthusiasts.
